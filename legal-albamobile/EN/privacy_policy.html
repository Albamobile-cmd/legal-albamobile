Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Data Controller

AlbaMobile (brand owned by Jorge López). Contact: info@albamobile.es

2. Purposes and legal basis

  • Authentication via Firebase Authentication to enable user login. Legal basis: pre‑contractual steps and consent.
  • Storage and sync in Firebase Firestore (e.g., performances, settings, statistics). Legal basis: service execution and consent.
  • Usage analytics with Firebase Analytics (aggregated/pseudonymised data) to improve the app. Legal basis: legitimate interest and consent where required.
  • Advertising with Google AdMob. By default, non‑personalised ads are shown until the user provides consent. Legal basis: consent.

3. Data processed

  • Account identifiers (email or sign‑in provider ID, where applicable).
  • App usage data (screens, events, preferences, statistics).
  • Advertising identifiers and technical device data (model, OS, language, truncated IP).

4. Processors and recipients

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics) – Google Ireland Limited. More info: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob – More info: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Retention

For as long as the account remains active or until the user requests deletion. Analytics logs are retained according to provider technical settings.

6. Rights

Users may exercise access, rectification, erasure, objection, restriction and portability by emailing info@albamobile.es. They may also lodge a complaint with their local data protection authority.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss or alteration.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The current version will be published on this site with the “last updated” date.
