Cookies Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. What are cookies?

Small files or similar technologies storing information on your device to enable app operation, remember preferences or perform analytics.

2. Cookies and technologies used

  • Essential: required for login and service delivery (e.g., Firebase session tokens).
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics to gather aggregated metrics and improve the experience.
  • Advertising: Google AdMob to display ads. By default, non‑personalised until consent.

3. Consent management

On first launch we ask for your privacy preferences. You can change them anytime in the app settings. You can also limit identifiers from your device settings.
