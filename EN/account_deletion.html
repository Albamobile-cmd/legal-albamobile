Account Deletion – Gestión de Actuaciones (A Contratiempo)

This page explains how users can delete their account and all related data in the Gestión de Actuaciones app (A Contratiempo – by Albamobile).

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from within the app:

  1. Open the app and sign in with your user credentials.
  2. Go to the Profile menu.
  3. Tap on “Delete Account”.
  4. Confirm the action. Your account and data will be permanently deleted.

Data that will be deleted

When you delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your user account (email and authentication data in Firebase Auth)
  • All your registered performances
  • Your profile information and preferences

Data retention

No personal data is retained after the account deletion. Anonymous usage statistics collected through Firebase Analytics do not allow individual identification.
